Service Process

The service process has been designed in a structured and user-friendly manner to ensure smooth handling of all applications submitted through the portal. Once a user submits the application form, the system first records the basic details and generates a reference for further processing.

After submission, the application enters verification stage where details are checked for accuracy. If any correction is required, users are informed for updates. This ensures only valid applications move forward.

Once verified, applications are processed in a systematic order to ensure fairness and consistency. Users receive regular status updates.

The system minimizes manual work and ensures fast, error-free processing through digital workflow.

Overall, the process ensures transparency, efficiency, and proper tracking at every stage.

Delivery & Processing

After verification and processing, final output is delivered through digital communication channels such as email or SMS depending on service type.

Users are updated regularly about status and completion progress.

Processing time depends on application type and verification requirements but is optimized for fast handling.

In case of additional checks, slight delays may occur.

Standard delivery timeline is 7–10 working days depending on service category.

System ensures transparency, reliability, and timely communication.
